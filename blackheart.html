inking is our life

“At Black Heart Ink passion comes first. Starting with your passion for the design you have chosen to the passion of our artists to create your vision perfectly.”

Black Heart Ink is a studio that produces the highest quality tattoos using the latest modern and traditional techniques and equipment. You will feel comfortable in a safe, clean environment in our studio. We know that our clients wear their tattoos from us with pride and you will too. As artists we are dedicated to the craft and tradition of tattooing, making it our goal to provide you with the highest standard of art that will last the test of time and you can wear and display for everyone to see.
